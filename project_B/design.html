Tattoo Design Process

step 1: brainstorm

think deeply about what you want for a tattoo. remember that tattoos are permanent, pick something that is either meaningful to you or something that you find visually appealing! it is always helpful to look online for inspiration!

step 2: style

consider how you want the tattoo to look. do you want a big or small tattoo? black and grey or color? thin or think strokes? there's a lot of stylistic aspects about tattoos that are important to consider while you design!

step 3: placement

think about where on your body you would like your tattoo. remember that certain places might be more sensitive than others (although this depends on the person). do you want a visible tattoo or one that is easily hidden? think about how the tattoo will harmonize with the shape of your body!

step 4: design!!

now it's time to transfer your ideas onto paper. sketch out your imagined tattoo and play around until it best suits your needs!

step 5: try it on

this step is typically done with a tattoo artist. they will print out the designs and tape them to the desired spot to give you an idea of what the tattoo will look like
